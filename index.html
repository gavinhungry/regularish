Regex Quick Reference
[a-z]Character from a to z
[0-9]Digit from 0 to 9
[xyz]x or y or z
[^x]Any character except x
(ab|xy)Match ab or xy
(...)Capture group
.Any character
\wA word character
\dA digit
\sA whitespace character
\bWord boundry
\x47Hex character 0x47
*0 or more
+1 or more
?0 or 1
{4}Exactly 4
{4,7}Between 4 and 7
{4,}4 or more
